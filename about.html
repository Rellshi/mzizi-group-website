About Mzizi Group of Companies

Mzizi Group is a dynamic logistics and supply chain management firm dedicated to facilitating seamless trade across Southern Africa. We specialize in the end-to-end management of import and export processes, bridging businesses in the SADC region with the global market.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable and deeply integrated logistics network in Southern Africa, recognized for simplifying the complexities of cross-border trade.

Our Mission

To empower African commerce by providing efficient, transparent, and cost-effective import and export solutions through strategic logistics planning and route optimization.

Our Core Values

  • Reliability: We deliver on our promises.
  • Efficiency: We minimize delays and maximize movement.
  • Integrity: Ethical and transparent compliance.
  • Connection: Building strong regional trade relationships.
